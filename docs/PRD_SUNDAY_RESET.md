# Sunday Reset

Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: v0.1  
Status: Draft

---

# 1. Vision

Sunday Reset is a calm, intentional weekly reset ritual designed to reduce mental chaos and help users enter the next week with clarity, peace, and direction.

It is NOT:
- a productivity app
- a task management system
- a habit tracker
- an AI wrapper
- a hustle-culture optimizer

It IS:
- a weekly ritual
- a mental reset
- an intentional planning experience
- a calm reflection system
- a reduction-of-overwhelm tool

The emotional goal is:

> “I no longer feel mentally scattered going into the week.”

---

# 2. Product Philosophy

The product should feel:
- calm
- minimal
- warm
- intentional
- emotionally grounding
- low-pressure
- reflective

The product should avoid:
- dashboards everywhere
- dense data visualization
- gamification
- notification spam
- productivity jargon
- “optimize yourself” energy

Primary framing:

> “Reset your week.”

NOT:

> “Maximize your productivity.”

---

# 3. Core User Problem

Many adults experience:
- Sunday anxiety
- mental clutter
- reactive weekly planning
- forgotten priorities
- emotional carryover from the previous week
- feeling directionless entering Monday

Current tools are often:
- too complicated
- too productivity-focused
- emotionally exhausting
- overloaded with features

Sunday Reset exists to:
- reduce mental noise
- create intentionality
- provide emotional closure to the previous week
- provide calm direction for the next week

---

# 4. Product Positioning

## Core Positioning

A weekly reset ritual for people who want clarity instead of chaos.

---

## Emotional Positioning

- clearing your desk before Monday

- taking a deep breath before the week starts

---

## Anti-Positioning

- over-engineering

# 5. Product Roadmap

# Version 0 — Early Access

## Launch Strategy

- Entire app free for first 6 months
- Early-access style release
- Goal is retention learning and emotional resonance validation
- No subscriptions initially
- No AI-guided system initially

Primary goal:

> Validate recurring ritual engagement and weekly return behavior.

---

## Version 0 Pricing

### During Early Access
- Free

### After Early Access
- One-time PWA unlock
- Target price: $2.99

Reasoning:
- low friction
- emotionally easy purchase
- impulse-buy territory
- avoids subscription fatigue

---

## Version 0 Technical Philosophy

Strong preference:
- frontend-only
- local-first
- minimal infrastructure
- no backend if possible

Preferred stack:
- React
- TypeScript
- Vite
- TailwindCSS
- Zustand
- Framer Motion
- localStorage
- PWA

No required:
- auth
- accounts
- cloud sync
- backend persistence

This reduces:
- operational burden
- infrastructure maintenance
- compliance concerns
- attack surface
- long-term complexity

# 6. Core User Experience

## Session Length

Target:
- 5–15 minute weekly ritual

---

## Ritual Flow

### Step 1 — Brain Unload

Prompt examples:
- “What’s still sitting in your head?”
- “What are you carrying into next week?”
- “What feels unfinished?”

Purpose:
- emotional decompression
- cognitive unloading
- reducing invisible mental load

---

### Step 2 — Reflection

Prompt examples:
- “What mattered most this week?”
- “What drained you?”
- “What gave you peace?”
- “What do you not want to repeat next week?”

Purpose:
- awareness
- reflection
- emotional processing

---

### Step 3 — Re-Center

Areas:
- family
- schedule
- meals
- finances
- faith
- relationships

Important:
This is NOT task management.

It IS:
- orientation
- alignment
- intentional preparation

---

### Step 4 — Choose the Week

Examples:
- top 3 focuses
- one thing to simplify
- one relationship to prioritize
- one thing to avoid

Purpose:
- intentional direction
- reduced overwhelm
- entering Monday with clarity

---

### Step 5 — Closure

The experience should end with:
- calmness
- closure
- direction
- emotional completion

Possible examples:
- “Your week has direction now.”
- “You do not have to carry everything at once.”
- “You have reset for the week ahead.”

The emotional tone of completion matters significantly.

# 8. AI Philosophy

# Version 0

No guided AI system.

Reasons:
- reduce complexity
- avoid operational burden
- avoid prompt costs
- validate ritual usefulness first
- ensure the app has value without AI

This is strategically important.

---

# Version 1 — Guided System

## Core Idea

Add optional guided reflections and summaries.

Important:
The feature should NOT be publicly framed as:
- “AI-powered”
- “AI coach”
- “AI therapist”
- “AI life assistant”

Instead, it should feel like:
- guided clarity
- thoughtful reflection support
- weekly insight
- guided reset assistance

---

## Possible Guided Features

Examples:
- weekly summaries
- recurring stress pattern detection
- emotional trend identification
- gentle reflection prompts
- simplification suggestions
- focus recommendations

---

## AI Philosophy

AI should:
- enhance the ritual
- not become the product

The app must remain valuable without AI.

---

## Version 1 Pricing

Optional guided subscription:
- target: $9.99/month

This unlocks:
- guided insights
- reflection assistance
- summaries
- enhanced reset support

The core app remains lightweight and affordable.

# 11. Risks

## Biggest Risk

The product accidentally becoming:
- another productivity app
- another journaling app
- another AI wrapper

If that happens:
- differentiation collapses
- emotional positioning weakens
- market fatigue increases

---

## Secondary Risks

- feature creep
- too much AI dependence
- over-engineering
- adding backend too early
- making the ritual feel like work

---

# 12. Strategic Advantages

Sunday Reset aligns strongly with:
- low infrastructure burden
- low operational complexity
- low compliance risk
- recurring user behavior
- emotional resonance
- mobile-first PWA strategy
- optional AI enhancement rather than dependency

It also avoids:
- enterprise sales
- SOC2 pressure
- direct Vega competition
- generic AI SaaS fatigue

---

# 13. Long-Term Potential

Sunday Reset could eventually evolve into:
- a ritual ecosystem
- an intentional living platform
- a calm digital wellness brand
- a family-oriented reset framework

However:

The product should resist unnecessary expansion.

Simplicity is one of the core differentiators.

- calm over clever
- ritual over productivity
- clarity over features
- emotional relief over dashboards
